About

Hi, I'm Marion!

I'm a freelance software developer and community lead in Zurich, Switzerland. I love solving problems and finding ways to write clean code. I have experience building web applications with Ruby on Rails, as well as HTML, CSS & JavaScript.

At the moment, I'm working with Hasura's GraphQL API and I'm learning how to build frontends with VueJS. I give presentations and workshops about GraphQL, and I will speak at some conferences about the topic.

I'm also a community person. I love interacting with the community and giving back. I co-founded Women Techmakers in Switzerland which is a platform for women in tech. We run workshops, organize conferences and social gatherings for people to network. Because there are by far not enough women in tech, I co-founded Ruby Monstas in 2015, a free weekly programming course for women that we still run every week.

I'm looking for...

Freelancing is great, especially because of the flexibility, but I'm looking for a new challenge. My dream job would be in developer relations. It's important that it's a technical position and it's important to me that there is a community aspect to it. I love to go to conferences, write blogposts and interact with the communities to find out what they need. If you know of something, please reach out to me :). In the meantime, I'm always open for freelance projects.
